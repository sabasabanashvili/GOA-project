About Us

Real Madrid Club de Fútbol, founded in 1902, is a professional football club based in Madrid, Spain. Known for its iconic all-white kit, the club is celebrated for its legendary players, monumental victories, and commitment to excellence both on and off the pitch.

Club History

Real Madrid's illustrious history is filled with unforgettable moments, from dominating the European football scene to creating a legacy that spans over a century. The club has consistently set benchmarks in the world of sports.
